# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective Date:** 03 June 2026

## Purpose
This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the rules and standards governing the use of the Private Premium 1-to-1 Communication Platform ("Platform"). All users are required to comply with this Policy when accessing or using the Platform. Failure to comply may result in temporary suspension, permanent access restriction, content removal, or legal action where applicable.

## Permitted Use
Users may use the Platform for lawful and legitimate purposes, including:
* Private communication
* Personal conversations
* Audio calls
* Video calls
* Photo sharing
* Video sharing
* Personal collaboration

Use of the Platform must always comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## Prohibited Activities
Users may not use the Platform to:

### Illegal Activities
* Violate any applicable law
* Promote criminal activities
* Distribute illegal materials
* Facilitate unlawful transactions

### Harassment and Abuse
Users may not:
* Threaten others
* Harass individuals
* Bully or intimidate users
* Engage in abusive behavior
* Promote hatred or violence

### Fraud and Deception
Users may not:
* Impersonate another individual
* Misrepresent their identity
* Conduct scams or fraud
* Distribute deceptive information

### Security Violations
Users may not:
* Attempt unauthorized access
* Exploit vulnerabilities
* Circumvent security controls
* Reverse engineer platform systems
* Interfere with service operations

### Malware and Harmful Content
Users may not upload, distribute, or transmit:
* Viruses
* Malware
* Spyware
* Ransomware
* Malicious software
* Harmful executable files

### Spam and Automated Abuse
Users may not:
* Send spam messages
* Perform automated messaging
* Conduct mass communication campaigns
* Abuse platform resources

## Content Responsibility
Users are solely responsible for all content they create, upload, transmit, or share through the Platform. This includes:
* Messages
* Photos
* Videos
* Audio Content
* Profile Content

The Platform does not assume responsibility for user-generated content.

## Privacy of Others
Users must respect the privacy rights of others. Users may not:
* Share private information without consent
* Disclose confidential information unlawfully
* Record or distribute communications in violation of applicable laws

## Intellectual Property
Users may not upload, distribute, or share content that infringes:
* Copyrights
* Trademarks
* Patents
* Trade Secrets
* Other intellectual property rights

Users are solely responsible for ensuring they have the necessary rights to any content shared through the Platform.

## Monitoring and Enforcement
The Platform reserves the right to investigate suspected violations of this Policy. Actions may include:
* Content Removal
* Temporary Restrictions
* Permanent Access Termination
* Cooperation with Law Enforcement
* Legal Proceedings where appropriate

## Reporting Violations
Users may report suspected violations through the designated support or compliance channel. Reports should include sufficient information to assist investigation.

## No Guarantee of Enforcement
While the Platform may investigate reports and enforce this Policy, it does not guarantee detection, prevention, or removal of every violation.

## Changes to This Policy
This Acceptable Use Policy may be modified from time to time. Updated versions become effective immediately upon publication unless otherwise stated. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of any updated version.

## Agreement
By accessing or using the Platform, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to comply with this Acceptable Use Policy. Failure to comply may result in suspension or termination of access to the Platform.
