# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 03 June 2026

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Private Premium 1-to-1 Communication Platform ("Platform", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, protects, and processes user information. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect
The Platform is designed to collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide its services. Information may include:

### User Information
* Display Name
* Profile Picture
* Unique Device-Based User Identifier

### Communication Data
* Text Messages
* Photos
* Videos
* Audio Call Metadata
* Video Call Metadata

### Technical Information
* Device Information
* Browser Information
* Operating System Information
* Network Information
* Error Logs
* Performance Metrics

## Information We Do Not Collect
The Platform is designed to operate without traditional account registration. We do not require:
* Username Registration
* Email Registration
* Phone Number Registration
* Social Media Accounts
* Public User Profiles

*(Unless voluntarily provided by the user)*

## How We Use Information
Collected information may be used to:
* Provide communication services
* Deliver messages
* Enable audio and video calls
* Synchronize devices
* Improve service performance
* Detect abuse and security threats
* Maintain platform stability

We do not sell user information to third parties.

## Media Retention Policy
* **Photos:** Photos may be automatically deleted from platform servers after 24 hours.
* **Videos:** Videos may be automatically deleted from platform servers after 24 hours.
* **Text Messages:** Text messages may remain stored until manually deleted by users or removed according to future platform policies.
* **Downloaded files:** Downloaded files remain on the user's device and are not affected by server-side deletion.

## End-to-End Encryption
Where implemented, communications may be protected using End-to-End Encryption (E2EE). Under E2EE:
* Messages are encrypted in transit.
* Media is encrypted in transit.
* Audio calls are encrypted.
* Video calls are encrypted.

*No encryption system can guarantee absolute security under all circumstances.*

## Cookies and Local Storage
The Platform may use:
* Local Storage
* Session Storage
* Essential Cookies

These technologies help:
* Maintain user identity
* Preserve user preferences
* Improve performance
* Enable platform functionality

The Platform does not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

## Data Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards including:
* Encryption
* Secure Communications
* Access Controls
* Infrastructure Monitoring
* Abuse Prevention Measures

*However, no internet-based service can guarantee complete security.*

## Sharing of Information
We do not sell, rent, or trade user information. Information may only be disclosed:
* When required by law
* To comply with legal obligations
* To protect platform security
* To investigate abuse or fraud

## User Rights
Depending on applicable laws, users may have rights to:
* Access their information
* Request correction of information
* Request deletion of information
* Restrict certain processing activities
* Receive information about stored data

Requests may be submitted through the official support channel.

## Children's Privacy
The Platform is not intended for children below the minimum age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect information from children in violation of applicable regulations.

## International Data Transfers
User information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions different from the user's country of residence. By using the Platform, users consent to such processing where legally permitted.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updated versions will become effective upon publication. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact Information
For privacy-related inquiries, requests, or concerns, users may contact the Platform operator through the designated support channel.

## Privacy Commitment
Our objective is to provide a private, secure, and minimal-data communication environment that prioritizes user confidentiality while maintaining reliable platform functionality.
