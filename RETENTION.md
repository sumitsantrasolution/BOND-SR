# Data Retention and Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** 03 June 2026

## Purpose
This Data Retention and Deletion Policy explains how the Private Premium 1-to-1 Communication Platform ("Platform") stores, retains, and deletes user data. The purpose of this Policy is to promote privacy, transparency, security, and responsible data management.

## Data Categories
The Platform may process the following categories of information:
* User Profile Information
* Text Messages
* Photos
* Videos
* Audio Call Metadata
* Video Call Metadata
* Technical Logs
* Security Logs

Each category may have a different retention period.

## Text Message Retention
Text messages are retained to support conversation history and continuity.

**Retention Period:**
* Stored until manually deleted by a user
* May be removed if required for legal, operational, or security reasons

Users remain responsible for their own content.

## Photo Retention
Photos are considered temporary media.

**Retention Period:**
* Automatically deleted from Platform servers after 24 hours from upload

Once deleted:
* Photos cannot be recovered by the Platform
* Photos may remain available on user devices if downloaded prior to deletion

## Video Retention
Videos are considered temporary media.

**Retention Period:**
* Automatically deleted from Platform servers after 24 hours from upload

Once deleted:
* Videos cannot be recovered by the Platform
* Downloaded copies remain under the control of users

## Call Data Retention
The Platform does not store call recordings unless explicitly stated and authorized. The Platform may temporarily retain limited call metadata for operational purposes, including:
* Call Start Time
* Call End Time
* Connection Quality Metrics
* Technical Diagnostics

**Retention Period:**
* Up to 30 days unless required longer for security or legal compliance

## User Profile Information
User profile information may include:
* Display Name
* Profile Picture
* Device-Based Identifier

**Retention Period:**
* Maintained while the user continues using the Platform
* Deleted when the associated identity is permanently removed

## Local Device Storage
Certain information may be stored directly on user devices through:
* Local Storage
* Browser Storage
* Application Cache

Such information is controlled by the user and may be removed at any time through device or browser settings.

## Security and Abuse Logs
Security logs may be retained for:
* Fraud Prevention
* Abuse Detection
* System Monitoring
* Platform Protection

**Retention Period:**
* Up to 90 days unless longer retention is legally required

## Automatic Deletion Procedures
The Platform operates automated deletion mechanisms. Deletion may occur:
* Immediately upon expiration of the retention period
* In scheduled maintenance cycles
* Through automated background processes

Deleted data may not be recoverable.

## User-Initiated Deletion
Users may request deletion of eligible information where technically feasible. Deletion requests may include:
* Profile Data
* Stored Messages
* Platform Identifiers

Some information may be retained where required by law or security obligations.

## Legal Retention Requirements
The Platform may retain certain information beyond standard retention periods when required:
* By law
* By court order
* For regulatory compliance
* For fraud investigations
* For security investigations

## Data Recovery Limitations
After permanent deletion:
* Recovery may not be possible
* Backup systems may eventually overwrite deleted information
* The Platform does not guarantee restoration of deleted content

## Policy Updates
This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, technical, legal, or security changes. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

## Commitment to Privacy
The Platform is designed around a privacy-first philosophy. Automatic media deletion, limited data retention, and user-controlled content management are core principles of the Platform's design.
